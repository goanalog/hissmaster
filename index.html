Loop design lab

Reference cards capture how duration, transport speed, and tactics reshape the tape texture. Use them while programming the deck.

Loop length

  • Micro (1–6s): Forms metallic drones and tonal pulses.
  • Short (8–15s): Accentuates rhythm and repeating gestures.
  • Long (45–180s): Masks repetition for evolving ambience.

Transport speed

  • Fast: Raises pitch, intensifies wow/flutter artifacts.
  • Slow: Drops pitch, accentuates tape wobble and bloom.
  • Manual sway: Hands on reels for subtle bends.

Tape tactics

  • Cover erase head ➜ sound-on-sound saturation.
  • External paths ➜ exaggerated length + manual scrubbing.
  • Möbius twist ➜ doubled playback with reverse bloom.
